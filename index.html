About Me

Since 2017, I am a Ph.D. candidate in computer science in Laboratoire d’Informatique, de Traitement de l’Information et des Systèmes (LITIS) in INSA Rouen Normandie, France, with a PH.D. thesis "machine learning and patterns recognition in chemoinformatics", focusing on graph kernels in machine learning and pre-image problems. I received the B.E. degree in information engineering, in 2014, and the M.E. degree in software engineering, in 2017, both from Xi’an Jiaotong University, China. For more information, please download my CV or visit my profile on LinkedIn.
