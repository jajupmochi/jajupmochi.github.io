About Me

Since 2017, I am a Ph.D. candidate in computer science in Laboratoire d’Informatique, de Traitement de l’Information et des Systèmes (LITIS) of INSA Rouen Normandie, France, under the supervision of Prof. Paul Honeine and Prof. Benoit Gaüzère, with the PH.D. thesis "machine learning and patterns recognition in chemoinformatics", focusing on graph kernels and graph edit distances in machine learning, and the graph pre-image problem. Before that, I received the B.E. degree in information engineering, in 2014, and the M.E. degree in software engineering, in 2017, both from Xi’an Jiaotong University, China. For more information, please download my CV or visit my profile on LinkedIn.

Research interests

- Preimage problems on graphs.

- Graph kernel within chemoinformatics.

- Graph edit distances.

Projects
The grant APi (2018-Now)
The grant APi (Apprivoiser la Pré-image) is funded by the French national research agency (ANR) for research of the preimage in machine learning for structured data. I worked on the pattern recognition on discrete structured spaces. I have submitted 1 journal paper [PP1], published 1 journal paper [J1] and 2 workshop papers [W1, W2], and published a library online available on GitHub (github.com/jajupmochi/graphkit-learn).
